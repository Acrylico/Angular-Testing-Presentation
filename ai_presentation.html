What is A.I., Really?

"The intelligence exhibited by machines or software."

A.I. is used in pretty much everything software related.

It can be anything from simple search logic and case selection all the way up to deep learning, planning, or the movement of machine parts.

Game of Life

Simple Rules to follow

  • Living cells with <2 living neighbors die
  • Living cells with 2-3 living neighbors remain alive
  • Living cells with >3 living neighbors die
  • Dead cells with 2 living neighbors spawns

The end result

Game of Life can generate several different patterns and repeating states

  • Boat
  • Frog
  • Glider
  • Pulsars

Swarming

Swarms have a set of goals they try to reach

  • Staying close to neighbors
  • Not getting *too* close to said neighbors
  • Heading in the same direction as their neighbors
  • Avoiding walls and obstacles
  • Avoiding predators or enemies

Pathfinding (A*)

Pathfinding using A* is a quick way to find the shortest route from point A to B.
It involves the use of a min-heap object and a hueristic to help steer the AI away from paths that are too long.
